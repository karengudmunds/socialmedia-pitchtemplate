Content Creation

Each month we will capture professional photos and short videos to provide enough content for you to fill your social media and website, and reflect the premier quality and variety of the your brand. This package includes full planning and co-ordination of each shoot, the photography itself and professional editing to suit your aesthetic.

  • 1x photoshoot per month with photography assistant
  • Up to 30 high resolution edited photos (enough for 1 month worth of content)
  • Putting together a curated instagram grid aesthetic, using the content provided.

Social Media Scheduling

Each month we would take the content we have shot and schedule it all for you in an instagram management tool (we recommend Later) creating a beautiful cohesive grid, and scheduling the posts at times where your audience is highly engaged. Then we would write engaging captions (in perfect english!), and add relevant hashtags and location tags.

We'll take into account current promotions, marketing strategies and featured products as well as the overall goals of each brand.

  • 30 posts per month scheduled to both Facebook & Instagram
  • Writing engaging captions
  • Relevant hashtags & location tags
  • Scheduled for automatic posting at key times
